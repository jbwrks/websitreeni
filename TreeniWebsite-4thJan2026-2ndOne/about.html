Our Story

Building sustainable businesses through expertise and technology

How We Got Here

Treeni was founded in 2015 with a simple belief: sustainability isn't just about compliance — it's about building businesses that thrive while protecting the planet.

Our founder, Ankush Bhatnagar, saw a gap: enterprises wanted to become sustainable, but they lacked both the expertise to know what to do and the tools to measure their progress.

So we started as consultants — working hands-on to build sustainability programs from the ground up. We learned what works in the real world, not just in theory.

The Turning Point

In 2019, we faced a crossroads. Rather than continue on autopilot, we stepped back, reflected, and rebuilt.

Starting in 2020, we reimagined Treeni. We assembled a team of sustainability professionals — domain experts who understand carbon accounting, supply chain, and climate risk.

We rebuilt our technology platform — resustain — to embody everything we'd learned. It's not a generic tool; it's a system that reflects how sustainability actually works in complex enterprises.

What We Believe

Sustainable business is good business

Companies that take ESG seriously build trust, attract talent, and create long-term value.

Sustainability is complex, but it shouldn't be complicated

Our job is to make the journey clear, actionable, and measurable.

Technology is an enabler, not a solution

The best software is useless without expertise. That's why we combine both.
